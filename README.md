## Healthcare Patient Waiting List Dashboard

This is a **Healthcare Analytics Dashboard** designed to monitor and analyze patient waiting lists across a hospital or healthcare system.

**Purpose**

The dashboard helps healthcare administrators and managers track how long patients are waiting for treatment, spot trends over time, and make data-driven decisions to reduce backlogs.

**What it covers**

The data spans 2018 to 2021, looking at two main patient categories — Inpatient (admitted to hospital) and Outpatient (treated without admission). It breaks down waiting lists by medical specialty and patient age groups.

**Key things it measures**

The core metrics are the average and median waiting list sizes, plus the current total number of patients on the waiting list at any given time.

**How it's structured**

It has two views: a Summary Page for a quick high-level snapshot of overall performance, and a Detailed Page where users can drill down into specifics by specialty, age group, or time period.

**Who uses it**

The primary users are healthcare leaders and domain experts — think hospital operations managers, clinical directors, or health service planners — who need visibility into patient flow and wait time performance.

In short, it's a tool built to answer one core question: *how well is the healthcare system managing its patient backlog, and where are the pressure points?*
